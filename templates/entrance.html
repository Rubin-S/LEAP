About LEAP Entrance Exam

The LEAP Entrance Exam is designed for students of Classes 10 and 11 who aspire to excel in competitive exams like NEET, JEE, and other undergraduate (UG) examinations. Conducted annually, this exam attracts over 1000+ students, out of which 300 top performers are selected for exclusive mentoring by students from NIT Puducherry. The mentoring sessions are held every Saturday and Sunday to prepare students for their dream careers.

Exam Details

  • Eligibility: Students of Classes 10 and 11
  • Exam Date: To be announced
  • Participants: Over 1000+ students annually
  • Selection: Top 300 students selected for mentoring
  • Mentoring: Conducted by NIT Puducherry students on Saturdays and Sundays
  • Purpose: Preparation for NEET, JEE, and other competitive UG exams
