The LEAP program is a beacon of excellence in our local community, driven by passionate students and mentors from NIT Puducherry. It empowers ambitious Class 10 and 11 students through rigorous training, regular screening tests, and personalized mentoring every weekend. Over 6 years, LEAP has consistently delivered outstanding academic results, producing numerous engineering and medical graduates who are now contributing to society.

About LEAP

Our Mission

To empower young minds of our local community with quality mentoring, skill-building, and academic excellence to unlock their full potential.

Our Vision

To be a leading youth mentorship program recognized for transforming students into confident, successful professionals and community leaders.

Our Values

Integrity, dedication, community service, collaboration, and commitment to student success guide everything we do.

Program Highlights & Benefits

Expert Mentoring

Experienced mentors provide personalized guidance and coaching to help students excel academically and personally.

Regular Screening Tests

Frequent assessments help monitor progress, identify gaps, and maintain high standards throughout the preparation.

Vibrant Community

A supportive learning environment fosters collaboration, motivation, and lifelong friendships among students and mentors.

How to Join LEAP

Eligibility

Open to motivated Class 10 and 11 students from the local community aiming for engineering or medical careers.

Application Process

Fill the registration form via the contact page and attend the screening test to qualify for the mentoring program.

Program Duration

The program runs year-round with weekend mentoring sessions and monthly screening tests to track progress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Motivated students currently studying in Class 10 or 11 interested in engineering or medical fields can apply.

Mentors are experienced NIT Puducherry students and alumni who demonstrate strong academic records and a passion for teaching.

Screening tests are monthly, designed to evaluate aptitude and subject knowledge in physics, chemistry, biology, and mathematics.

Click the “Register for Next Year Entrance Test” button above or visit the contact page to fill out the registration form.
